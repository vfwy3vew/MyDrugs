🟠 What is MyDrugs?

MyDrugs is a fully anonymous marketplace where you can browse, order, and track a variety of products — safely and discreetly.

🔐 How do you protect my privacy?

We do not log IPs or user activity. All communication is encrypted. For full anonymity, we recommend using Tor and cryptocurrencies.

💸 What payment methods are accepted?

We currently accept Bitcoin (BTC), Monero (XMR), and Ethereum (ETH). Transactions are confirmed within minutes once the network validates them.

🚚 How does shipping work?

Orders are packed in stealth packaging and shipped within 24–48 hours. Delivery time varies by region but typically takes 3–7 business days.

📦 What is “stealth shipping”?

Stealth shipping means your package looks like a normal parcel with no signs of its contents. We use vacuum-sealed and layered protection.

❌ What if my package doesn't arrive?

We offer reship for orders with proof of non-delivery. Full refunds are not available. Always provide a valid and complete address.

🧾 Is there a minimum order?

Yes. Minimum order value is $50 (USD equivalent in crypto). Bulk orders receive automatic discounts.

🧠 How do I stay safe?

Use Tor, do not share personal data, always double-check wallet addresses, and use PGP encryption for sensitive messages.
